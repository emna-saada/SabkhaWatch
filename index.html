Featured solution

Smart buoy for real-time salinity monitoring

Problem: Salinity and water-level changes in sabkhats are often detected too late because field measurements are irregular.
Solution: Deploy solar-powered smart buoys that measure salinity, conductivity, temperature, and water level, then send readings to Sebkha Watch.
Impact: More frequent monitoring supports faster response to drought stress, pollution events, and ecological imbalance.
Call to Action: Fund a 6-month pilot with 5 smart buoys and technical mentorship from IoT partners.
